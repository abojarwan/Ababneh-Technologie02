    Explore our best laptops & 2-in-1 PCs and decide which one you'd want to watch out for. Find deals on high-performing personal laptops, legendary gaming systems, and exceptional business laptops.

    High-performance laptops
    XPS laptops offer stunning visuals, best-in-class aesthetics, exceptional build quality, and powerful features. These high-performing laptops come in 13-inch, 15-inch and 17-inch displays. If you are in the market for top Dell PCs this coming holiday season, watch out for deals on XPS laptops & 2-in-1s.

    Gaming laptops
    Dell will have a wide range of offers when you're shopping for a Black Friday gaming laptop. Alienware laptops have stunning designs, high performance, and premium features for the most immersive gaming experiences. G Series laptops deliver powerful CPUs, stunning graphics, and what you need for split-second responsiveness and immersive gameplay.

    Everyday laptops
    Inspiron laptops and 2-in-1s are great devices for staying connected through email and social media. They are also great for streaming music and watching movies. With robust memory and storage and the latest Intel® and AMD processors, Inspiron laptops combine thoughtful, compact design with features and options that fit your life. Available in 11-inch to 17-inch screens, Inspiron laptops are great laptops to watch out for when Black Friday computer deals go live.

    Business laptops
    Vostro laptops are designed with small businesses in mind. They have the essential features and provide advanced security through anti-virus protection and fingerprint readers.

    Latitude laptops and 2-in-1s come with best-in-class features for business users. Get increased bandwidth on the go with Wi-Fi 6E or cellular options . With the latest processors and innovative technology, Latitude intelligently powers any business.

    Workstations
    Precision mobile workstations computers are designed specifically for those who handle processing or graphics heavy applications like 3D modeling, data analysis, video editing and much more. Precision workstations feature premium design and professional-level components to power intense workloads.

    Maximize your savings: 3 tips for shopping Black Friday laptop deals

    Black Friday laptop deals can help you save hundreds or thousands of dollars on Dell products. Here are a few tips to prepare for Black Friday.

    Tip #1: Look for early deals
    Check Dell.com regularly for updates and announcements about doorbusters and early access – the best laptop deals may be available on the days leading up to Black Friday. Sign up for email alerts to get the latest news on offers and deals!

    Tip# 2: Make a plan
    Black Friday laptop deals go fast, so you have to act decisively to get the specific gear you want. Knowing the type of laptop, features, and options you want is essential, and that requires a little research before Black Friday rolls around.
    First, do your research on XPS, Inspiron, and other Dell laptop brands. Once you've decided on which Dell laptop, you'll want to know your ideal specifications - screen size and type, processor, graphics cards, and amount of RAM. From there you can keep an eye out for deals on specific laptop models and configurations come Black Friday.

    Tip #3: Maximize Black Friday savings
    Expand your research and prepare to take advantage of more than just one product offer. Browse through electronics, accessories, and other items that will work well with your chosen laptop!
